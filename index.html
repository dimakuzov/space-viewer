Scene Editor

View Mode:
WASD - Move
Q/E - Up/Down
Mouse - Look
Click to lock cursor

Edit Mode:
Click to place cubes
Click on objects to select/delete
Hotkeys:
1 - Add Cube
X/Del - Delete
V - Toggle GLB visibility
C - Toggle Collider Edit
Tab - Toggle Mode

Collider Edit Mode:
WASD - Move Collider
5cm per movement
