View Mode:
WASD - Move
Q/E - Up/Down
Mouse - Look
Click to lock cursor
Click panels with blue border to open URL
Edit Mode:
Click to place panels
Click on panels to edit text & URL
Click on objects to select/delete

Panel Edit Mode:
Edit text and URL (optional)
Panels with URLs have green borders
WASD - Move Panel Horizontally
R/F - Move Panel Up/Down
Ctrl+Enter - Save Changes
Escape - Cancel Editing

Collider Edit Mode:
WASD - Move Collider
Q/E - Move Collider Up/Down
R/F - Scale
T/Y - Rotate
